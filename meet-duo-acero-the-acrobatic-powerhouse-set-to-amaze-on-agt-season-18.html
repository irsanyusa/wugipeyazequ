Meet Duo Acero, The Acrobatic Powerhouse Set to Amaze on 'AGT' Season 18

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In the next episode of America’s Got Talent, viewers are in for a treat as they witness the awe-inspiring performance of Duo Acero. This dynamic duo, comprising Edison Acero and Giselle Souza, showcases their incredible strength and acrobatic skills, leaving both judges and the audience speechless.

Duo Acero Brings Acrobatics Combined With Pole Dancing On AGT

The main act of Duo Acero revolves around a pole, where Edison Acero demonstrates his exceptional physical strength by holding onto the pole, positioning himself perpendicular to the structure. Meanwhile, Giselle Souza displays her agility and balance by gracefully maintaining a handstand on Edison’s side.

As seen from a short video teasing their performance, the crowd erupts with cheers and applause. The judges, Sofia Vergara and Heidi Klum, are visibly impressed by the duo’s extraordinary talents. Adding to the heartwarming spectacle is a little girl, likely the daughter of the acrobatic pair, sitting on the judge’s lap.

Duo Acero’s showstopping audition stands out among the diverse array of impressive acts renowned by AGT. Their remarkable skills in hand balancing, aerial straps and hoops, and also Chinese pole acts set them apart from the crowd, ensuring they leave a lasting impression on the audience.

With their undeniable talent and captivating act, it’s clear that Duo Acero belongs to the grand stage of AGT. As the reality competition series celebrates the incredible range of various talents, from physical stunts to comedy, dance, and singing, Duo Acero’s unique and awe-inspiring performance promises to resonate with viewers across the nation.

Duo Acero Regularly Performs During Live Shows or Circuses

Duo Acero, seasoned professionals, have earned their stripes through numerous performances in circuses around the world. From dazzling audiences across Europe to wowing spectators under the big top, they have consistently delivered awe-inspiring acts that certainly leave spectators breathless.

Hosted by the charismatic Terry Crews and featuring executive producer Simon Cowell as one of the judges, alongside Howie Mandel, Vergara, and Klum, AGT continues to entertain and surprise audiences in its 18th season.

Make sure not to miss the opportunity to witness Duo Acero’s fantastic audition, as they soar to new heights with their acrobatic feats. Tune in to AGT on Tuesday, August 1, to catch their whole performance, and also stay tuned for more incredible auditions as Season 18 continues to unfold on NBC this summer.
