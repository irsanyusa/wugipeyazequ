Angie Griffin's biography: age, measurements, cosplay, is she married?

Angie Griffin is an American cosplay artist, YouTuber, actress, and social media personality. She is well known for the YouTube channel Screen Team that she runs with her husband, Chad Nikolaus.

Popularly known for The Amazing Spider-Man song on YouTube, Griffin has acquired fans worldwide. She is charismatic, talented and leads a secretive life. So how much do you know about Angie Griffin's biography?

Career

Angie began the YouTube channel Screen Team with her husband Chad Nikolas on the 11th of January 2010. The couple shares a love for entertainment; this is evident in their YouTube channel's description, which reads:

A real-life couple that makes geeky videos at home!

The most viewed video on the channel is the 2012 upload titled Spiderman In Real Life Song (For Kids) that currently has 151 million views. Screen Team currently has 1.67M subscribers.

Angie is also an actress and producer. She has been featured in the following:

  • Bloodwood Cannibals (2010)
  • Famous (2011)

The entertainer appeared in these before she started her YouTube career. Presently, she is concentrating on YouTube content creation.

Cosplay

Angie Griffin is a fan of cosplay and parody. In her Screen Team channel, she has made parodies of popular movie characters. One of her most viewed videos is her rendition of the Marvel superhero character, Spiderman.

Her videos have been well received, prompting Griffin to do more cosplays of current hype trends. She has done a cosplay of the popular video game Resident Evil. She also dresses up as movie characters and posts photos on her social media pages.

Is Angie Griffin married?

Many fans of the cosplay artist have wondered about the marital status of the internet diva. So is she married?

Yes, Angie Griffin is married to Chad Nikolaus. The couple has been together since 2006.

Chad Nikolas is a popular actor who has appeared in:

  • Walking in Circles (2014)
  • Famous (2011)
  • Movie Mob (2009)

Chad is also a cosplay fan, as he co-stars alongside his wife in the parody cosplay videos on their YouTube channel Screen Team.
