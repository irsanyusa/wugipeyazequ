Knicks basketball player shot, robbed outside strip club

Cleanthony Early was reportedly shot and robbed outside of a Queens, New York, strip club in the early hours of Wednesday, December 30.

The New York Times reports that, 24-yr-old Early, had left the CityScapes Gentlemen’s Club at about 4 a.m. this morning with a woman and had been in an Uber when the incident took place.
