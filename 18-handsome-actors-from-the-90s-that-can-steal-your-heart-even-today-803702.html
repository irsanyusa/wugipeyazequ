18 Handsome Actors From the 90s That Can Steal Your Heart Even Today

The 1990s gave us a lot of movies, TV shows, and bands that really handsome men were members of. Remember young Jude Law or Robbie Williams? The mix of charisma and talent helped them and other young men from this list remain idols for many people all around the world.

We at Bright Side decided to stroll down memory lane and remember the most famous men of the ’90s that are still really charming today. And in the bonus section, you will see a hot man from The Clone.

Nick Carter (41 years old)

Nick Carter is the youngest member of the Backstreet Boys.

Robbie Williams (47 years old)

In the 1990s, Robbie Williams became famous as the singer and songwriter of Take That.

Matt Dillon (57 years old)

In 1998, the actor played the leading role in Wild Things.

Kyle MacLachlan (62 years old)

From 1990 to 1992, the actor portrayed Dale Cooper in Twin Peaks.

Nick Lachey (47 years old)

At the end of the ’90s, Nick Lachey was a member of 98 Degrees, which made him famous.

Andrew Keegan (42 years old)

The actor is famous for his role in 10 Things I Hate About You.

Devon Sawa (43 years old)

Devon Sawa is a Canadian actor that portrayed Casper as a person.

JC Chasez (45 years old)

JC Chasez is the singer from NSYNC, the group Justin Timberlake was also a member of.

Christian Slater (52 years old)

Christin Slater portrayed a detective in Interview with the Vampire: The Vampire Chronicles.

Brandon Quinn (43 years old)

The actor became famous thanks to his role in Big Wolf on Campus.

Ricky Martin (49 years old)

Ricky Martin is the singer of the famous hit, “Livin’ la Vida Loca.”

Sébastien Roch (48 years old)

The French actor and singer became famous thanks to his role as Christian from Helen and the Boys.

Thiago Lacerda (43 years old)

This is a Brazilian actor that portrayed one of the main roles in Terra Nostra.

Karl Urban (49 years old)

Before he appeared in The Lord of the Rings, he was in Xena: Warrior Princess.

Jude Law (48 years old)

In 1997, the actor appeared in the drama, Wilde, which was a blast for his career.

Bryan Adams (61 years old)

Bryan Adams is a Canadian rock musician and the singer of “(Everything I Do) I Do It for You.”

Patrick Puydebat (50 years old)

The French actor became famous thanks to the role of Nicholas in Helen and the Boys.

Facundo Arana (49 years old)

This Argentinian actor portrayed one of the main roles in Wild Angel.

Bonus: Luciano Szafir (52 years old)

You might remember this actor thanks to his role in The Clone. He was only 33 when the show was on the air.
