Louis Tomlinson's Documentary, 'All of Those Voices' Is Coming to Paramount+

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by JC Olivera/Getty Images

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Former member One Direction, Louis Tomlinson is ready to take us on an intimate journey through his life and music career with the upcoming documentary, Louis Tomlinson: All of Those Voices. This exciting documentary originally premiered in March, offering fans a candid look into Tomlinson’s experiences as he ventures into the world of solo stardom.

Louis Tomlinson: All of Those Voices is Coming to Paramount+

Paramount+ recently revealed that All of Those Voices will soon be available for streaming on their platform. For Tomlinson, this film holds immense significance, as he expressed in a press release.

“This film means everything to me, and I’m looking forward to having it out there in the world!” Tomlinson said.

Directed by Charlie Lightning, All of Those Voices provides an up-close and personal glimpse into Tomlinson’s life post-One Direction, a band that captured the hearts of millions but went on hiatus in 2016. The documentary showcases Tomlinson as he embarks on solo endeavors, including a global tour and the creation of his second solo album, Faith in the Future, released in 2022.

Tomlinson, who narrates the documentary himself, has shared the joyous news that All of Those Voices will be available for streaming on Paramount+ starting from October 4 in the United States and Canada. Fans around the world won’t have to wait long, as the documentary will also become accessible in the U.K., Australia, Latin America, Brazil, Italy, France, Germany, Switzerland, Austria, and South Korea on the following day.

The documentary’s London premiere in March brought together Tomlinson’s fellow One Direction member, Liam Payne, who took to Instagram to share his feelings about the film and his admiration for Tomlinson. Payne’s heartfelt post conveyed how the opportunity to see the world through Tomlinson’s eyes moved him deeply.

The Documentary’s Trailer Has Been Released

Tomlinson’s journey from self-doubt to solo success is a story that fans have been eagerly anticipating, and the newly released trailer for Louis Tomlinson: All of Those Voices gives us a glimpse into this remarkable transformation.

The trailer takes us on a nostalgic ride, featuring archival footage of a young Tomlinson as a confident schoolboy and his rise to fame as part of the chart-topping boy band, One Direction. However, behind the scenes, Tomlinson grappled with self-doubt, doubting his ability to succeed without his bandmates.

Yet, the trailer also reveals a turning point in Tomlinson’s life, a moment of self-discovery that led him back to the studio. With newfound confidence and the support of loved ones, he embarked on his solo journey, finding purpose and fulfillment.

The trailer doesn’t just dwell on the past; it also offers a glimpse into Tomlinson’s present as a successful solo artist. We see him performing for adoring crowds worldwide and also enjoying the happiness that comes from pursuing his passion.
