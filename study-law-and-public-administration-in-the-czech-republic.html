Study Law and Public Administration in the Czech Republic - Live &amp; Study

27. 10. 2023 | Fields of Study

When considering the number of businesses, offices, organizations, and institutions, one thing is certain. Experts in law and public administration will always be in high demand. Companies and organizations seek experts who can give legal advice, understand business-related issues, and are reliable at the same time. In this article, we will zero in on some options to study law and public administration in the Czech Republic. There are some great ones.

Institute of Technology and Business in České Budějovice

At the Institute of Technology and Business in České Budějovice, you can study Business Administration. This program provides the students with important skills to meet the needs of the current job market. They learn about economics, management, marketing, accounting, and much more. The school also offers an MBA program in English (Master of Business Administration). Located in the south of the country, České Budějovice is a very pleasant place to study.

Faculty of International Relations, Prague University of Economics and Business

The Faculty of International Relations offers an interesting Bachelor’s program in English – International and Diplomatic Studies. The students are equipped with a lot of knowledge in economics, law, and statistics. At this school, you can also study a Ph.D. program in Commercial and International Economic Law. It’s a great option to become an expert in the field of both national and international business.

Faculty of Economics and Management, Czech University of Life Sciences in Prague

If you wish to study business administration, you can’t go wrong with the Faculty of Economics and Management of the Czech University of Life Sciences in Prague. You can study Business Administration in the Bachelor’s program. There is an interesting follow-up Master’s program European Agrarian Diplomacy. You can also study the Ph.D. program Regional and Social Development. With this school, you can become an expert in the agrarian sector. The graduates also have a deep background in business and gain managerial capabilities, so much needed nowadays.

Anglo-American University in Prague

The Anglo-American University in Prague is another great possibility for you. There are a lot of interesting options to choose from, such as International Relations: Human Rights, International Relations: International Law, International Relations: Security Studies, Political Science: Comparative Law, or Political Science: Studies in Social and Political Development. You can also study an LLM program in International Intellectual Property Law.

All of these schools are a great investment in the future. Studying fields related to law and business is something worth looking into. You will have a lot to offer after graduating. To learn more about studying law and public administration in the Czech Republic, click here.
