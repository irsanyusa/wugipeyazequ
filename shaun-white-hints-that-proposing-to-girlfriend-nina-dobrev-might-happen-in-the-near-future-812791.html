Shaun White Hints That Proposing to Girlfriend Nina Dobrev Might Happen in the Near Future

Finding love is a beautiful experience, and the story of Nina Dobrev and Shaun White is a perfect example of this. Their journey together shows that true love exists, and anyone can find it, even if it takes some time to understand it.

Many years passed between when they started to date and when they first met.

Shaun White, the Olympic gold medalist and professional snowboarder, and Nina Dobrev, the actress known for her roles in The Vampire Diaries, have been in a relationship since early 2020. They met a long time ago, in 2012, but their romantic story began in those 8 in-between years.

The couple first sparked dating rumors in early 2020 when they were seen together at an event in Malibu. However, it wasn’t until Nina Dobrev posted a photo of them on Instagram in April of that year that their relationship was confirmed. Since then, the couple has been going strong, sharing sweet posts about each other on social media, and attending events together.

Suddenly they found love.

Despite their busy schedules, the couple makes time for each other, often traveling together and supporting each other’s careers.

“Shaun and Nina have really been enjoying living together. They’re super compatible and both free-spirited people. They’re very comfortable around each other and both supportive of one another in their fields,” a source says.

Fans have been speculating about whether the couple might be getting engaged soon and actually Shaun has given us lots of reasons to think so.

Shaun gives signals that he wants to make it official.

Recently White opened up in an interview about their relationship and decided to drop a hint that he has some serious plans.

“Nina and I are really happy, and we’re enjoying our time together. We’re just enjoying our time together right now. No pressure. But I told her, I was like, ’Look, after the Olympics, I finally got my freedom to do whatever. And let’s just have fun and be together and support each other.’”

Who knows, maybe we will soon welcome a newly married couple.
