Roger Daltrey (Singer) - On This Day

Profession: Singer and Founder of the Rock Band "The Who"

Biography: Roger Daltrey is an English singer, best known as co-founder and lead singer of The Who. The band have had a number of hit songs, including: "My Generation," "Won't Get Fooled Again," and "You Better You Bet."

Outside of The Who, Daltrey has also had a prolific solo career. He has released ten studio albums, also with a number of hits: "Giving It All Away," "Without Your Love," "Under a Raging Moon," etc.

Born: March 1, 1944
Birthplace: East Acton, London, England
Age: 80 years old

Generation: Silent Generation
Chinese Zodiac: Monkey
